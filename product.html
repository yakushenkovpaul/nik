      Overview of Villa Qabalah in Si Sunthon, Phuket

      Villa Qabalah is a house and villa project located in Si Sunthon, Phuket and it is scheduled for completion in Jun 2023. It has 31 units across 2 floors and was developed by DNA collection Company Limited

      About Villa Qabalah

      Connect the pristine nature’s power with the authentic finest living at Villa Qabalah, where the project name derived from an ancient Hebrew Language from the Holy Bible that is related to humans and nature, to realize the importance of nature with human life. The project also allows you to experience and spend your life with peaceful sustainable living by providing comprehensive facilities, including a healthcare cafe, yoga room, sauna, meeting room, lush green garden, and CCTVs surrounding the project. Additionally, it provides 3 different types of villas that are ideal for both compact and large families. Moreover, each villa comes with 2-4 bedrooms, with a private pool and lush garden on the rooftop that will connect you to nature daily.

      Notably, Villa Qabalah is located in a tranquil location in the Bangjo-Banya area that is surrounded by lush green nature and airly purified. The project is also conveniently accessible from Blue Tree, Boat Avenue Cherngtalay, Robinson Lifestyle Thalang Phuket, Porto De Phuket, Tesco Lotus Thalang, Bang Tao Beach, Layan Beach, etc., which are ready to facilitate the residents' needs perfectly. And now, are you ready to charge your life batteries with pristine nature for your peaceful and happy life? Let’s experience our privileges at Villa Qabalah.

      Project Information
      Villa Qabalah is located at 8/58, Moo 7, Banya-Bangjo Road, Si Sunthon, Thalang, Phuket. This pool villa comprises 31 units on the plot size of 7.3 Rai that was developed by DNA Collection Company Limited and is sceduled to be completed in 2023.

      Villa Types and Details
      - Moss: A 3-story villa with 2 Bedrooms and 2 Bathrooms
      - Ferb: A 2-story villa with 3 Bedrooms and 3 Bathrooms
      - Pine: A single-story villa with 4 Bedrooms and 4 Bathrooms

      The living area ranges from 237 - 620 sq. m., and the plot size starts at 37.50-102 sq. m., with a private swimming pool and a rooftop garden for each unit.

      Direction to the Project
      To get to the project by car, you can start from Blue Tree by heading southeast onto Si Sunthon Road (Number 4025) for about 250 meters, then turn left onto Route 4015 to drive further for about 750 meters. Then, turn left again and continue straight for about 240 meters. Afterward, turn left onto Soi Bangjo 1, drive further for about 1.2 kilometers, and you will find the project on the right.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      All kind of pets are allowed at Villa Qabalah including dogs, cats, rabbits, hamsters, guinea pigs, birds, and reptiles.
      Bang Tao Beach is the nearest Beach from Villa Qabalah and it is 3.8 km away.

      The building (the bricks and mortar) can be owned by a non-Thai national outright in their name in what is called the house registry which secures ownership indefinitely of the structure. 

      In Thailand non-Thai nationals cannot own land outright in their name. Land can be controlled through either a Thai Company or a long-term registered lease. The longest registered lease term by Thai law is 30 years and most developers will offer 3 terms for a total of 90 years.

       

      Read more here

      A 30-year lease period is legally protected under Thai law and ownership cannot be disrupted. It is common for developers to offer an additional two terms of 30 contractually obligating a total of 90 years. 

      Prior to purchasing a leasehold property it is important to secure a copy of the lease agreement or get further clarity on these three points.

      #1 - Who is the lessor? (an individual or a Thai Company) Securing a lease from a Thai Company offers much more security than a private individual. 

      #2 - Do I have voting rights as a Lessee? (Some lease contracts do not allow lessee to have voting rights)

      #3 - Is there a succession clause in the lease agreement that will allow inheritance of the lease?

      The surrounding area, where Villa Qabalah is situated, offers a wide variety of restaurants. See the closest of them below:
